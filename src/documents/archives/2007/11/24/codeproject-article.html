If you are a Visual Studio developer or hobbyist (like myself) you probably have heard of CodeProject.  For those that haven't:

The Code Project was formed to provide developers with a place to meet and exchange ideas. We hope to provide developers with all the resources they need to help them in their day to day programming, as well as helping them keep up to date with the latest technologies.

- About The Code Project

At CodeProject users submit, discuss, and rank articles pertaining to Visual Studio and .NET programming.  It is a great resources that I check very often.  They have articles from beginner to advanced so you are bound to find something to fit your skill level.

Why am I writing this now?  Because I just gave back to the CodeProject by submitting my first article.  I submitted my post from a few days ago (Blog Post, CodeProject Post).  Submitting the story to CodeProject was a breeze and within a few hours I received my first comment.   I hope to be able to do it again in the future.
