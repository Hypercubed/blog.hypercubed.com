My blog is worth $6,209.94. How much is your blog worth? Business Opportunities Weblog has a little applet that uses Technorati's API to compute and display your blog's worth using the same link to dollar ratio as the AOL-Weblogs Inc. deal. This is based on research by by Tristan Louis's research into the value of each link to Weblogs Inc.
