--- title: "Save Flash from Firefox" post_name: "save-flash-from-firefox" guid: 'http://www.hypercubed.com/wordpress/?p=96' status: "publish" date: '2005-10-19 20:32:00' post_id: '96' tags: [ 'Firefox' ] --- The other day I posted an article on how to place a flash file for display on your desktop. I mention a couple of methods for saving the flash file to your hard drive. Both methods involve examining the source code to get the flash file URL. Turns out there is a much easier way in Firefox. I'll leave the details to the source but basically, you can view a list of all media on the page, including flash files, from the "View Page Info" menu. Once you find the appropriate item in the media list there is a "Save AsÂ…" button that allows you to easily store the file to your HD. Very nice stuff.
