  • Digging Alexa - how the geek demographic doubled overnight | BlogCadre - In April I noticed that my daily reach (number of users visiting hypercubed sites per million) dropped to nearly negligible according to Alexa. I first I thought my site was wiped off the internet map.  But no, my sites were still accessible.  Then I thought that maybe on of the sites that link into mine had dropped me but I couldn't figure out which one.  Eventually I gave up and accepted the fact that nobody likes Hypercubed anymore.  Turns out it was due to the sudden adoption of Alexa toolbar by a huge number of Digg users.  Suddenly, overnight, the Alexa rankings were altered by the new demographic.  Tech sites shot up and non tech sites shot down.  I always considered hypercubed a tech site... guess I was wrong.  Next week I'm going to have all cute kitten links.
